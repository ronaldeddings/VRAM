## 1. Key Observations

### The "Oral vs. Written" Conflict
There is a fundamental tension between **Ron's natural processing style** and his **system's architecture**:

## 2. Deep Insights

### The "Ghost in the Machine" is Ron
The "Empty" folders (Family, Health, Finance) are not evidence of neglect; they are evidence of **misaligned tooling**. Ron is heavily engaged in these areas, but the *data* is trapped in the "Work" transcripts.
*   **Hypothesis:** Ron doesn't write in his "Gratitude Journal" because he already expressed his gratitude verbally to Emily or Brandy in a meeting 20 minutes prior. The system fails to "count" this as a valid entry because it's in the "wrong" file format (`.mp3`/transcript vs `.md`).
