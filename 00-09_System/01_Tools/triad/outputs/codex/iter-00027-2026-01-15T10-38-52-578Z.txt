**Insights**
- There’s a strong morning grounding habit but little end‑of‑day consolidation, which likely reduces learning capture and feedback loops.
- Sleep appears to be a major energy lever; many “Today I Feel” entries link energy to sleep debt, and average logged sleep is under 6 hours (e.g., ` /Volumes/VRAM/30-39_Personal/30_Reflection/30.01_daily/2025-10-09.md`, ` /Volumes/VRAM/30-39_Personal/30_Reflection/30.01_daily/2025-10-12.md`).
- Template dashboards reference `LifeArchitecture/Tasks` and `Goals` that aren’t present, creating silent failures that undercut momentum.
- Capture > processing imbalance: the system records a huge volume of audio but shows little evidence of extraction into goals, learning, or reviews.

**Opportunities**
- Convert the diffuser/coffee ritual into a lightweight anchor habit with a single checkbox or 1‑line log.
- Standardize health metrics (especially `sleep_quality`) to enable real trend tracking.
- Add a weekly “audio triage” step to transform 1–3 snippets into learning notes or actionable items.
- Reduce evening friction: a 90‑second “wins/lesson/next” entry twice a week would close the loop.
